Every puppy is different. Some puppies might sleep all day, while others might never stop playing! You should get to know your puppy well before bringing him home with you! Below are some things you should consider before adopting a puppy


  1. Do I have enough time to spend walking my puppy?
  2. What if my puppy damages some of my belongings? Am I patient enough to teach my puppy manners?
  3. Do I have enough space in my home for a puppy?
